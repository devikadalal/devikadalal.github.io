Sticky Briefs is a creative way to spend 5 minutes. Each week we post a new mini-brief from a new Creative Director - written on a sticky note. Then, we encourage our creative community to respond to the brief and send in their ideas - also on sticky notes. After a week our judge picks a winner. It's up to you what you do with that idea next.

FAQ

  1. Who can enter?

    Sticky Briefs is open to everyone.

  2. When do new briefs come out?

    New Sticky Briefs are usually posted on Mondays.

  3. When are submissions due?

    Sticky Brief submissions are due by the Friday the brief was posted. Winners are announced on the Sunday.

  4. Who are the judges?

    A different Creative Director each week.

  5. What does the winner get?

    A confidence boost.

  6. How do I enter Sticky Briefs?

    Share or DM us your idea on Instagram or Twitter
    with #stickybriefs.
